The open source deadman switch
What is it?

A deadman switch is a device/piece of software that performs an action after the operator's death.

Why should I care?

Everyone dies one day. And whether you believe in the after life or not, you may want to leave a message for people dear to you.

Custom delivery times

Do you wish your message to be sent 27 days and 6 hours after you die? Go ahead.

Multiple options for checking whether you're alive

As of now we offer E-mail or pressing a button inside of the web app as the available methods

Open Source

AfterWill is just an instance of Epilogue, the open source backend developed by us. Anyone is free to host it.

Fair pricing

We don't think you should go broke while paying something you can't even see in action while you're alive.
